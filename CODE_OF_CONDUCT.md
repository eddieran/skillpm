# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on ideas.
- Provide actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or doxxing.
- Disruptive behavior that harms collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or moderate content that violates this policy and may restrict participation for repeated violations.

## Reporting

Please report conduct issues privately to project maintainers.
